Teach Someone Else

Formula Sheet Creation

Practice Exams & Feedback Loop Strategy

1. Collect Resources
Gather past exams, old quizzes, and practice problems from professors, TAs, study groups, or online resources.
2. Simulate Real Conditions
Do at least one full practice exam per class under actual timed conditions without notes or aids.
3. Analyze Mistakes
Grade honestly, track every mistake, and convert them into targeted 30-60 minute study sessions.
4. Seek Feedback
Get input from peers or instructors on recurring errors. Record the top 3 mistakes for each class and create specific improvement plans.

Exam Day Success Checklist

  • Timing & Location: Confirm time & location, arrive 15–20 minutes early
  • Essential Items: Student ID, allowed calculators, multiple pens, water, tissues, post-exam snacks
  • Study Materials: Bring notes if allowed; otherwise a clean summary sheet for last-minute recall
  • Morning Routine: Do a 20-minute light review (no new material), eat a balanced breakfast
  • Anxiety Management: Use 4-4-8 breathing technique for 2–3 minutes before starting the exam
